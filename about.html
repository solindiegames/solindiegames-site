About Solindie Games

A small indie studio with big ambitions and a love for thoughtful game design.

Passion meets pixels.

Solindie Games is a team of 2 based in Sydney, Australia. Carlos is the designer and artist who makes the games look beautiful. Tim is the programmer and writer who makes the games function.

We’ve been playing games since the NES and Sega Master System were considered cutting edge. If we’re not playing games, we’re making games or watching content about games. Our goal is to build games that offer truly unique experiences.

Small team, big ideas.

Solindie Games is built on the belief that creativity scales faster than headcount. We focus on tight, handcrafted experiences—worlds with texture, mechanics with intent, and stories that feel lived‑in rather than manufactured.

Every project is an experiment in atmosphere, playfulness, and emotional punch, shaped by a team that obsesses over the details most studios overlook.
