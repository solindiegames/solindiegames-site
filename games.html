Ultra Mega Planet Battles

Arcade Shooter Available Now

Ultra Mega Planet Battles is an intense arcade-style, space-based, twin-stick shooter.

Stop the evil Brutes from taking over Earth and the Solar System. Players can play by themselves, or with up to 3 friends local co-op or Steam Remote Play (total of 4 players). Select your character, select and modify your ship, then get shooting!

Available on Steam (for PC) and Google Play (for phones and tablets, single-player only). Links below.

Sanctum Falls

Survival Horror Coming Soon

A quiet town, a sudden death, and a truth that refuses to stay buried. After receiving a devastating call, the protagonist arrives to settle their sibling’s affairs — only to find small, unsettling details that don’t add up. Their search for answers pulls them through Sanctum Falls, revealing a community tangled in ritual, secrecy, and something far darker than grief.

Side‑scrolling exploration, light combat, puzzle‑driven investigation and fast‑travel across the town all feed into an atmospheric, episodic mystery where every location pushes you closer to the cult lurking beneath Sanctum Falls — and the spirits they’ve awakened.
