Spacer Shooter X

Arcade Shooter Browser Available Now

Space Shooter X drops you into a classic vertical-scrolling dogfight reimagined with a striking particle-driven look. Enemies pour in wave after wave, each one faster and deadlier than the last. Master mouse-aimed combat, manage weapon heat before overload locks you out, and fight through celebrations between waves as the pressure builds.

With glowing thrusters, laser-guided aim, and explosive particle feedback on every hit, it’s arcade action you can feel. Rack up your best run, submit your initials to the leaderboard, and see how deep into the waves you can go.

Sanctum Falls

Survival Horror Windows Coming Soon

A quiet town, a sudden death, and a truth that refuses to stay buried. After receiving a devastating call, the protagonist arrives to settle their sibling’s affairs — only to find small, unsettling details that don’t add up. Their search for answers pulls them through Sanctum Falls, revealing a community tangled in ritual, secrecy, and something far darker than grief.

Side‑scrolling exploration, light combat and puzzle‑driven investigation all feed into an atmospheric, episodic mystery where every location pushes you closer to the cult lurking beneath Sanctum Falls — and the spirits they’ve awakened.
